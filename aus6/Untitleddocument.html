FINDINGS,HMDA,PREVIOUS SUBMISSION

Findings

  • Under the "Findings" section, users can access the findings from the Result section after successfully running DU/LPA/Both DU/LPA. 
  • DU Findings contain the response from FannieMae, while LPA Findings contain the response from FreddieMac. 

HMDA

  • On successful run of DU/LPA/both DU & LPA, that particular recommendation will be update to HMDA Form in encompass 

Previous submission

  • Upon selecting the "Previous Submission" tab, users will be redirected to a page where they can view the following details:
  • Submission entries
  • Date and time of submission
  • Status
  • Recommendation
  • Additionally, users can access the DU/LPA results of the entries by clicking the "View" option. They can also view changed values during the submission by selecting the "Show Changed Values" checkbox.
  • Users can find the Rep and Warrant PDF, as well as the findings, in the result section.
